About Us

The Bosss Music Distribution & Marketing

THE BOSSS MUSIC DISTRIBUTION & MARKETING was established in 2022. Since our launch, we have successfully worked with artists both within and outside Nigeria who required professional music distribution and digital marketing services.

Our mission is to help independent artists build sustainable careers by increasing visibility, expanding audience reach, improving streaming performance and strengthening artist branding across major digital platforms.

As we continue to grow, we look forward to expanding our services globally and helping more artists achieve long-term success in the music industry.

Core Values

  • Transparency: Clear communication and reporting.
  • Excellence: Delivering top-tier results in every campaign.
  • Dedication: Treating every artist's brand as our own.
