The Boss Music Distribution & Marketing Professional Artist Services That Get You Results

Whether you're just starting your journey or already building a serious brand, The Boss Music Distribution & Marketing is here to take your career to the next level. Our all-in-one service model is designed to help you grow your audience, increase your exposure, and turn your talent into a lasting legacy. Below is a breakdown of the services we offer, each built to provide real value for independent artists, record labels, and music entrepreneurs.

1. Google Ads

We run highly targeted Google Ads campaigns to promote your music, videos, website, or merchandise. These ads appear across Google search results, YouTube, and partner websites, reaching fans who are actively looking for new content. This is a strategic and proven way to boost your visibility and get your content in front of the right audience — not bots, just real people with real interest.

2. Artist Website Biography

Your website is your official online presence — your personal digital headquarters. We build sleek, professional websites for artists that include music streaming links, bio, photos, videos, tour info, and merchandise. A strong website builds credibility with fans, event organizers, and industry professionals. It's also a key asset for press, bookings, and search engine visibility.

3. Google Knowledge Panel & Wikipedia Setup

Having a Google Knowledge Panel and Wikipedia page gives your brand authority and trust. We help you create and verify your artist identity on both platforms, making it easier for fans, journalists, and labels to find official and accurate information about you. This service is especially powerful for artists who want to be taken seriously at an industry level.

4. Song Claim Under Artist Profile

When your song is released, it should appear under your official artist profile on platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube. We handle the process of claiming and assigning your music properly, so there’s no confusion, lost streams, or missing credits. It ensures your brand stays consistent and professional across all platforms.

5. TikTok Influencer Campaigns Using Your Sound

TikTok is one of the fastest ways to break a song today. We partner your sound with real TikTok influencers who will create authentic, engaging videos using your music. These influencer collaborations help your track gain exposure, drive trends, and increase streams on all platforms. It's a smart strategy for organic reach and building momentum quickly.

6. Music Artwork and Business Flyers

Your visuals are just as important as your sound. We provide high-quality, customized cover art for singles and albums, as well as professional flyer designs for events, releases, and marketing. Whether you need clean and minimal or bold and graphic-heavy, our designs match your aesthetic and communicate your brand clearly.

7. Lyrics Video Creation

We create lyric videos that help fans connect with your music while making your content more shareable on platforms like YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram. These videos are professionally synced to your song and designed with your branding in mind. They are perfect for promotion, fan engagement, and building a visual presence online.
